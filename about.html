Annie Provost grew up and attended both primary and secondary schools in Rockland. She was called to the Bar of Ontario in September 2009 after earning her Bachelor of Law (LL.B.) degree and her Bachelor of Arts (B.A) degree in Psychology from the University of Ottawa.

Annie articled in Thunder Bay and returned to Rockland to practice law in 2009. Annie is fluently bilingual in French and English and provides legal services in the areas of family law and wills and estates.

Annie is licensed by the Law Society of Ontario and she represents clients in the Rockland (Prescott-Russell) and Ottawa areas and appears before the Superior Court of Justice in L’Orignal, Ottawa and Cornwall. Annie also accepts Legal Aid certificates in the area of family law.

Annie was appointed to be a Notary Public in the Province of Ontario in November 2010 and she has been operating her own law firm since October 2016.
