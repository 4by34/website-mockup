Wills and Estates

Wills and Powers of Attorney are oftentimes an area of law that people ignore or push to a later date. However, this is an area of law that can have serious repercussions on your loved ones in the event of your passing or an incapacity. Annie makes herself available to help her clients prepare the proper documents and to answer any and all questions that you may have regarding your wills and powers of attorney.
