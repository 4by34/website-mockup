FAMILY LAW

Family law is a complex field and it is important that people understand the various issues, their rights and the consequences arising from their choices before making agreements and/or starting court proceedings. Annie is available to help her clients navigate the complexities of family law. Annie can also help you and inform you of your rights when you are starting a relationship (cohabitation and/or marriage) or in the event of a breakdown of your relationship (separation).

Services include:

• Adoption

• Child support

• Cohabitation Agreement

• Custody and access

• Division of property

• Divorce

• Marriage Contract

• Parenting Plan

• Separation Agreement

• Spousal support
