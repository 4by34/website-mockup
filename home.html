Federal Pardon Waiver Services assists individuals in obtaining a Canadian Pardon (Record Suspension) by removing a past criminal offence from public record in Canada. We also help individuals obtain U.S. Entry Waivers and Arrest Record Purges (Criminal Record Expungement).

Our Pardons Canada head office is based out of Toronto, Ontario where we have over 20 years of combined proven experience in providing our clients a hassle-free Pardons and Waivers services that ensures that the highest standards for trust are set and maintained! This has made Federal Pardon Waiver Services the fastest growing and largest Pardon and Waiver providers in Canada.

By staying up-to-date with all the legislative and procedural changes concerning both Canadian Pardon (Record Suspension) and U.S. Entry Waiver applications and by working on and submitting nearly 10,000 applications per year, we have the experience and competence to provide the fastest and most accurate applications, thus allowing our clients to travel freely and use their skills and capabilities to enrich their lives to the fullest, without the hindrance of a past mistake!

Every one of our clients is special and so too are their needs. We take great care to make sure that your unique situation is understood and that your application is completed and presented in the best possible light to ensure the best possible outcome! You will never be asked to pay extra for expedited services because all of our clients applications are a priority.
